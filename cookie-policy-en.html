What are cookies

Cookies are small text files, made up of a string of letters and numbers stored in the browser when using websites or applications. They allow the manager of the website to be able to identify you among the other visitors on website. They can not be executed as code or used to spread viruses and can not allow access to the user's hard drive. Note that if you disable cookies altogether, the website SITOWEB may not operate properly.

Types of cookies and used for purposes

These cookies are technical in nature and allow the website to work correctly. How to check and delete cookies

Most web browsers accept cookies, however the user can change the settings of the web browser in order to refuse cookies, disable the existing ones or simply being informed when a new cookie is sent to your device. Moreover, through these operations you can change your agreed preference. Here below we indicate the activities to be done in order to check and disable cookies depending on the browser used. Some features of the website may be reduced or not available by disabling cookies.

Google Chrome

– In the setup menu, select "Show Advanced Settings" at the bottom of the page
– Select the button "content settings" on the privacy section
The section at the top of the page which appears after explains cookies and allow you to select the cookies you would like. It also allows you to delete all cookies currently stored.

For further information on how to delete cookies using Chrome:

https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en&p=cpn_cookie

Mozilla Firefox

– In the tools menu, select "Options"
– Select the "Privacy" button in the "Options" folder
– From the dropdown menu choose "Settings History". This will show you the options for cookies and you can choose to enable or disable them by clicking on the box.

For further information on how to delete cookies using Firefox:

https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookie-website-preferences?redirectlocale=en-US&redirectslug=Enabling+and+disabling+cookie

Internet Explorer 6+

– In the menu bar, select "Internet Options"
– Click on the button "Privacy"
– You will see a cursor of privacy settings which has six settings which allow you to check the number of cookies which will be placed: Block all Cookies, High, Medium High, Medium (default level), Low, and accept all cookies.

For further information on how to delete cookies using Internet Explorer:

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-vista/block-or-allow-cookie

Safari browser

– In the Settings menu, select "Preferences" option
– Open the Privacy section
– Select the desidered option from the "Block cookies" section

For further information on how to delete cookies using Safari:

https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201265

All the other browser

For information about managing cookies through other browsers, consult your documentation or on-line help file.
